About the Cleveland Tech Survey

Cleveland is a special place. Lots of the information provided by survey-type sites (Glassdoor, Stack Overflow, etc.) doesn't seem to accurately represent Cleveland. So the Cleveland Tech Survey is an attempt to help us learn more about what it's like to work in tech in Cleveland.

If you would like to offer feedback about the site, email here. Keep in mind that this is a work in progress.

Pull requests are welcome here.

Donations of any amount would be helpful. This isn't a team. I'm just one guy trying to do something nice for the community I live in.

Thanks!
